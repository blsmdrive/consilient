Getting Started

Welcome to Consilient! Here's how to get the most out of your new thinking tool.

Creating Your First Folder

To create a folder on the canvas:

  • Tap and hold anywhere on the canvas
  • Choose a color for your folder
  • Give it a meaningful name
  • Start adding notes!

Adding Notes

Inside any folder, tap the + button to create a new note. You can:

  • Format text with bold, italic, and headers
  • Add images and file attachments
  • Apply tags to connect ideas across folders

Using Tags

Tags are the magic that connects your ideas. When you add a tag to a note, Consilient automatically:

  • Assigns a unique color to each tag
  • Shows connections between folders with shared tags
  • Makes all tagged content instantly searchable

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my notes across devices? +

Consilient uses iCloud to sync your notes across all your Apple devices. Make sure you're signed into the same iCloud account on all devices and have iCloud Drive enabled. Your notes will sync automatically in the background.

Can I export my notes? +

Yes! You can export individual notes as text files or export entire folders. Long-press on any note or folder and select "Export" from the menu. You can choose to export as plain text, markdown, or PDF.

How do I restore deleted notes? +

Deleted notes are moved to the Archive and can be restored within 30 days. Access the Archive from the main menu, find your note, and tap "Restore" to bring it back to its original folder.

Is my data secure? +

Absolutely. All your notes are stored locally on your device and encrypted. If you use iCloud sync, your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We never have access to your notes - they're completely private to you.

Can I customize folder colors? +

Yes! Long-press on any folder and select "Edit" to change its color. Choose from our curated palette or create a custom color that represents your content perfectly.
